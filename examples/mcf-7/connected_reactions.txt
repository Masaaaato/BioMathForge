EGF binds EGFR <--> EGF_EGFR
EGF_EGFR dimerizes <--> EGF_EGFR-EGF_EGFR
EGF_EGFR-EGF_EGFR is phosphorylated <--> EGF_EGFR-EGF_EGFR_p
EGF_EGFR-EGF_EGFR_p activates PI3K
PI3K phosphorylates PIP2 --> PIP3
PI3K phosphorylates AKT --> AKT_p
AKT_p phosphorylates GSK3B --> GSK3B_p
EGF_EGFR-EGF_EGFR_p phosphorylates Shc --> Shc_p
Shc_p binds Grb2 <--> Shc_p_Grb2
Shc_p_Grb2 binds SOS <--> Shc_p_Grb2_SOS
Shc_p_Grb2_SOS activates RAS_GDP
RAS_GDP <--> RAS_GTP
RAS_GTP activates RAF1
RAF1 phosphorylates MEK --> MEK_p
MEK_p phosphorylates ERK --> ERK_p
ERK_p translocates <--> ERK_p_nucleus
ERK_p phosphorylates RSK --> RSK_p
RSK_p phosphorylates cFOS --> cFOS_p
cFOS_p is degraded
PP2A_act dephosphorylates ERK_p --> ERK
PTEN dephosphorylates PIP3 --> PIP2
